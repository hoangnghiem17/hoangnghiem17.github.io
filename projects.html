🎓 Master Thesis

Chatbot Development for Sustainable Waste Disposal

📄 Thesis PDF 📁 GitHub

Description: Developed and compared chatbots using Google Dialogflow, Rasa and a custom LLM-based implementation (GPT-4o-mini via LangChain and LangGraph) to assist Frankfurt citizens in sustainable waste disposal.

Technologies: Google Dialogflow, Rasa, LangChain, LangGraph, GPT-4o-mini, FAISS, Python

Key Learnings: Evaluation of chatbot platforms, NLU, dialogue management, deployment strategies, multimodality, RAG implementation, tool orchestration

Accomplishments: Comprehensive comparison of chatbot platforms, successful implementation of LLM-based agent with advanced features

🏆 Data Mining Cup

Machine Learning for Zero Waste - Frankfurt

📰 News Article 🏆 Competition

Description: Analysis to support the city of Frankfurt to become a certified Zero Waste City. The results were presented to the board of the city of Frankfurt and in the Data Mining Cup where we won the first place.

Technologies: Python, KNIME, Classification algorithms, Explainable AI, ARIMA, Prophet

Key Learnings: Data mining for sustainability, classification techniques, time series forecasting, explainable AI, presenting to stakeholders

Accomplishments: 🥇 1st Place in Data Mining Cup 2023 (69 participants from 17 countries), results presented to Frankfurt city board
